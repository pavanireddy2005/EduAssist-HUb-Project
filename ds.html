Data Science is the field of extracting insights from structured and unstructured data using scientific methods.

  • Combines statistics, programming, and domain expertise
  • Used for prediction and decision-making
  • Works with large-scale datasets

Data Collection is the first step of the Data Science pipeline.

  • Databases
  • APIs
  • Web scraping
  • Surveys and logs
  • IoT sensor data

Data Cleaning improves the quality and accuracy of the dataset.

  • Handling missing values
  • Removing duplicates
  • Outlier detection
  • Normalization & Encoding
  • Fixing incorrect formats

EDA helps understand the structure, patterns, and relationships in the data.

  • Summary statistics
  • Correlation analysis
  • Detecting patterns and anomalies
  • Histograms, scatterplots, heatmaps

Statistics is the backbone of Data Science for analyzing variation and probability.

  • Descriptive vs Inferential Statistics
  • Probability distributions
  • Hypothesis testing
  • Correlation & covariance
  • Bayesian concepts

Machine Learning is used to build predictive and classification models.

  • Regression models
  • Classification models
  • Clustering algorithms
  • PCA & dimensionality reduction

Visualizing data helps interpret trends and communicate results clearly.

  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
  • Plotly
  • Dashboards (Power BI / Tableau)

Big Data tools manage massive datasets that do not fit in normal memory.

  • Hadoop
  • Spark
  • Hive
  • Kafka
